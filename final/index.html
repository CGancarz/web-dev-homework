M I S C H A

Date with a dog





Hi there! My name is Mischa Barkton. I am a five year old German Shepherd Mix that currently resides in Montana's Bitterroot Valley with my people and older Labradork brother Sprite. I was born on a farm in central Illinois and brought home Thanksgiving weekend of 2014. Even though the rest of my human family had no idea I was going to be joining them, I won over every person I met with my good manners, quiet personality, floppy ears and tiny piranha teeth! I remember being SO scared of my puppy brother when I first met him because he was much bigger and louder than me - I even tried to hide behind the couch just to get away from him! Luckily, it took no time for the two of us to become the best of friends. My puppy brother Sprite and I are absolutely inseperable, whatever he does or wherever he goes - I am right behind him to check out things and keep a close eye on him. After a few weeks
Likes: SNOW, my brother Sprite, chicken in any form, snooping into the refrigerator, sucking on my paw while asleep, long walks & hikes, my Chuckit water skimmer and decrepit football, sleeping in bed until I get too hot, car rides and road trips, windsurfing out the window in the car, paw massages, having my ears scartched, KITTIES!, malt balls, and questioning foods before I eat them.
Dislikes: other dogs, the neighboring horses, soft foods, deer in my yard, when my people play basketball and the ball bounces, getting baths, and when my ball rolls under the couch.
